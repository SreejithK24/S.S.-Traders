About S.S. Traders

Our journey began in 1996, right here in Manuguru. Recognizing a critical gap in the local market, the founders of S.S. Traders embarked on a mission. Extensive market research revealed that businesses in our region faced significant challenges in sourcing industrial gases, often having to travel over 100 kilometers to secure their supplies. This firsthand understanding of local needs became the bedrock of S.S. Traders, establishing us as a trusted name in industrial, medical, and specialty gases. Our focus has always been on quality, safety, and customer partnership, delivering solutions that power your industry and support our community.

Meet Our Founder: Laxmi Prasad Chava

Driven by a vision to serve the local industrial community of Manuguru and beyond, Laxmi Prasad Chava, our first-generation founder, established S.S. Traders with an unwavering commitment to reliability and customer-centric service. The early challenges, including the arduous task of sourcing materials from distant locations, only strengthened this resolve. This experience shaped the company's core ethos of perseverance, dedication to meeting client needs, and ensuring that essential resources are always within reach for our partners, including vital local industries.

"Our goal was simple: to make essential industrial resources accessible and to build lasting partnerships based on trust, consistent supply, and a genuine understanding of our customers' operational needs."

- Laxmi Prasad Chava
